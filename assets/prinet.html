Prinét

Event Identity

A surreal, circus-like art event hosting a variety of artists and performers, inviting the audience to look beneath the seemingly nonsensical and find meaning in the work that is in front of them.

Taking inspiration from Dada, Russian Constructivism, and Japanese EroGuro Nonsense art movements, the imagery reflects a hypnotic and theatrical experience.
The name Prinét is a meaningless word that is intended to sound fancy.
