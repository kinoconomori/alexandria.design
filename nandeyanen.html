Nandeyanen!

Print

“Nandeyanen” (なんでやねん) is a slang phrase from the Kansai region of Japan that translates to something like “What the hell!”

Nandeyanen is a scrapbook of my personal experiences studying abroad at Osaka University of Arts in spring 2017.
