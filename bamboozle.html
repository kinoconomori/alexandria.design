Bamboozle

Packaging

The Amazing Mister Bamboozle has opened up his marvelous workshop after years of experimentation, and you are invited to join in the fun! Open up the box and discover a world of mystery and wonder!

Bamboozle is an imagined line of toys that play with the expectations of the audience. Although labelled simply as 'teddy bear' or 'doll', each of the toys are actually a frankensteined version of multiple toys reassembled together.
Bamboozle's logo and identity is based on the idea of a playful patchwork quilt, with colors and patterns assembled together to hint at the idea of the reassembled toys and create excitement.
