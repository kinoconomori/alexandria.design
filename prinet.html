Prinét

Event Identity

A surreal, circus-like art event hosting a variety of artists and performers, inviting the audience to look beneath the seemingly nonsensical and find meaning in the work that is in front of them.

Taking inspiration from Dada, Russian Constructivism, and Japanese EroGuro Nonsense art movements, the imagery reflects a hypnotic and theatrical experience.
The name Prinét is a meaningless word that is intended to sound important.

The wordmark is pieced together from letters of different size and style to reflect the slightly chaotic mix of art and ideas at the show.
Each attendee is given a conceptual workbook filled with seemingly nonsensical tasks in relation to the gallery space, as well as the general event information on the front transarency page. The pages of the book are printed on paper of different pattern, texture, and weight.
